AB Acute kidney injury(AKI)is a common severe syndrome with high
   mortality,which can progress to chronic kidney disease. Early risk
   assessment,accurate prediction,and prompt diagnosis are crucial in the
   management of AKI. Advancements in biomarkers and machine learning based
   on big data have made it possible to predict AKI. This article provides
   a summary of the application of biomarkers and machine learning in AKI
   prediction.
Z4 急性肾损伤(acute kidney
   injury,AKI)是一种常见的重症综合征,可进展为慢性肾脏病,病死率高。在AKI的诊治环节中,早期风险评估、准确预测、提前诊断最为关键。目前
   更多的AKI新型生物标志物被发现,重症医疗也进入了大数据时代,利用生物标志物和基于大数据的机器学习方法预测AKI成为了可能。文章将对如何应用生物
   标志物和机器学习预测AKI进行综述。
